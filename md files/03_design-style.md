# Design Style Guide

## Philosophy

Clarity over decoration.  
Function over trends.  
Calm, confident, focused.

Inspired by:
- Scandinavian minimalism
- B2B SaaS clarity
- Product-first startups

---

## Color Palette

Primary:
- Charcoal Black (#111111)
- Soft White (#FAFAFA)

Accent:
- Nordic Blue (#3A6EA5) or Muted Teal
- Neutral Gray (#8A8A8A)

Avoid:
- Neon colors
- Heavy gradients
- Overly playful palettes

---

## Typography

Primary Font:
- Inter / SF Pro / IBM Plex Sans

Headings:
- Medium to Semi-bold
- Tight line-height

Body:
- Regular weight
- High readability

---

## Layout Principles

- Large whitespace
- Single-column focus
- Clear hierarchy
- Minimal animations

No carousels.  
No fake testimonials.  
No clutter.

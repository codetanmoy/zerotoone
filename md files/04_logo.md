# Logo Design Direction

## Style

- Wordmark or minimal symbol
- Flat, no gradients
- Works in black & white

## Characteristics

- Serious
- Technical
- Trustworthy
- Founder-led

## Do Not Use

- Abstract crypto-style icons
- Over-engineered symbols
- Trend-driven logos

## Usage

- Website
- Pitch decks
- Documents
- Email signatures

The logo should feel comfortable next to:
Volvo, Ericsson, SAP, Siemens.
